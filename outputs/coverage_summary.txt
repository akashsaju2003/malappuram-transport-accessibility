Public Transport Accessibility Study – Malappuram
Coverage Summary


Total Population (Raster): 3,178,661

Served Population (within 500m of bus stops): 510,565
Served Percentage: 27.77 %

Unserved Population (outside 500m of bus stops): 2,660,096
Unserved Percentage: 72.23 %

Interpretation:
- Only 27.77% of Malappuram’s population lives within 500m of a bus stop.
- The majority, 72.23% (≈ 2.66 million people), remain underserved.
- This highlights significant accessibility gaps in public transport coverage.
- Areas identified as unserved can guide planning for new bus stops or improved routes.
